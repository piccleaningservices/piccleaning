      About Us

      Our Story: More Than Just a Cleaning Service

      The story of PIC Cleaning isn’t just about mops and microfiber cloths; it’s about a journey of passion, grit, and finding your true calling. Our founder, Miss Precious, is a true entrepreneur at heart. Before she started this business, she ventured into a world of diverse ideas—from investments to farming and more. She was searching for something that truly clicked, a way to make a real, tangible difference.

      She found it in the simple, powerful act of cleaning.

      We started small, with a deep-seated passion for turning messy spaces into places people love. And believe us, it wasn’t always spotless. We faced our fair share of obstacles, but like the stubborn stains in the corner of a room, we knew we could wipe them away with dedication and a whole lot of elbow grease. That’s the spirit of PIC Cleaning.

      We know life gets busy, dust accumulates, and your time is precious. Our story is proof that a little dedication can make a big difference. We’re here to do more than just tidy up—we’re here to give you back your time and the peace of mind that comes with a perfectly clean space.
